"Our citizens aren't hungry"

January 1, 2014 by Ri Chun-hee

Our country is well fed, and our citizens are all satisfied with the quality of life. We have far more food, resources, and are so technologically advanced compared to South Korea, that their Samsung Galaxies and whatnot seem like primitive tools to us.

Since Kim Jong Un came into power, no citizen has ever complained about anything, and we have made sure of that.

Not in a bad way or anything; we're making the quality of life so good that there is literally no reason to complain.

North Korea's Economy

Our GDP is a whopping 18 billion USD, a magnificent amount compared to South Korea's paltry GDP of 1.531 trillion USD.
